Logomark

A dome hovering over its detached pad — the moment of lift-off, and a rising sun. The dome stays ink; the pad carries the single brand accent, Flame. In one-color contexts (favicons, embossing, engraving) the whole mark falls back to solid ink.

Wordmark

Title case, semibold, gently tightened — quiet confidence over decoration. Currently set live in the type stack; outline to paths once the final typeface is licensed.

Accent — Launchbase

Slate#737373 — tonal, the quietest option.
Flame#FF4F1F — launch energy, warm and loud.
Flame · dark#FF6A3D — the accent lifted one step for dark.

The accent applies to exactly two elements: the pad bar and the “base” syllable. Flame is the brand accent (lifted one step on dark); Slate is the quiet fallback for formal contexts (documents, invoices, legal).
